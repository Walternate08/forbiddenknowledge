The Swedish Fish Society is a secret society dedicated to the appreciation and study of Swedish fish candies. Our members are passionate about this beloved confectionery and gather in secret to share their knowledge, indulge in their favorite treats, and uncover the secrets of the Swedish fish's origin and significance.

The BJU Tunnels

The BJU Tunnels are a complex system of underground passageways that are rumored to exist beneath the city of Bob Jones University. Little is known about their origin or purpose, and they have remained hidden from the public eye for centuries. Some believe that they were created by a secret society known as the "The Swedish Fish Society" for unknown reasons, while others speculate that they hold ancient relics or forbidden knowledge. The truth remains elusive, and the BJU Tunnels continue to be a subject of fascination and curiosity among explorers and researchers alike.
